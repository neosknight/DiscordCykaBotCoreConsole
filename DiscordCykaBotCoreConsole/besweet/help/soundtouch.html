this manual lists all the available switches under the -soundtouch( ) section of BeSweet v1.5.
for more info, read the BeSweet FAQ !

pay attention : switches are case-sensitive.

Switches
-tempo
 Changes the sound to play at faster or slower speed than original, without affecting the sound pitch.

example : "-tempo n" will change sound tempo by n%   (n=-95..+5000)
-pitch
 Changes the sound pitch or key, without affecting the sound tempo or speed.

example : "-pitch n" will change sound pitch by n semitones (n=-60..+60 semitones)
-rate
 Changes both the sound tempo and pitch, as if an LP disc was played at wrong RPM rate

example : "-rate n" will change sound rate by n %  percents  (n=-95..+5000)
-r
 Convert frame rate without changing pitch

example : "-r 25000 29970" will convert a 25.000fps stream (PAL) to a 29.970fps stream (NTSC)
-quick
 Use quicker tempo change algorithm

gain speed, lose quality..
-naa
Don't use anti-aliasing filtering

gain speed, lose quality...
